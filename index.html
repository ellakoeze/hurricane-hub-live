other information you might want

The source for the storms on this page comes from a list compiled by the Hurricane Research Division of NOAA of hurricanes that have made landfall on the continental U.S.. The storm years, months categories and windspeeds also come from the same source. The storm rainfall data comes also comes from NOAA on this page. The historical hurricane tracks come from another NOAA maintained tool.

Other data for storms is included in the downloadable files (though much of it is incomplete). Sources for that data are as follows:

  • Maximum central pressure: NOAA
  • Adjusted cost in millions: NOAA
  • Official deaths: NOAA
  • Average forward speed in knots: Cyclone Potential Damage Index project
  • Maximum forward speed in knots: Cyclone Potential Damage Index project
  • Average size in nautical miles: Cyclone Potential Damage Index project
  • Maximum size in nautical miles: Cyclone Potential Damage Index project
  • Maximum storm surge in feet: SURGEDAT
  • Maximum storm tide in feet: SURGEDAT
